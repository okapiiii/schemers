The ailing King has no heir, barbarians at the gate, peasantry in revolt. Petty lords war over petty greivances.

Who will climb the ladder for ultimate power, and who will fall?

Schemers is a narrative strategy video game, in which players compete for power in the royal court.

INFLUENCE, EXPLOIT, COERCE: Gain leverage over characters and use their abilities.
SCHEME AGAINST YOUR FOES: Play Schemes and manipulate the court to amass power and outwit your enemies.
KINGDOM ON THE BRINK: The kingdom lurches from crisis to crisis, exploit the situation at your peril.

  • Turn-based Singleplayer
  • Simultaneous Multiplayer
  • Customize your character and your family dynasty
  • Customize your kingdom and its people
